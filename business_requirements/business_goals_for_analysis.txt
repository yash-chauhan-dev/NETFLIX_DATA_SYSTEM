** Business Goals for Analytics

1️) User Engagement & Watch Behavior

- How many users are active daily, weekly, and monthly?
- How much time do users spend watching content?
- What are the most-watched movies and TV shows?

2️) Content Performance Analysis

- What genres are the most popular?
- What are the top-rated movies & TV shows?
- What content is trending in the last 7 or 30 days?

3️) Subscription & Revenue Metrics

- What is our Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)?
- What is our churn rate (users canceling subscriptions)?
- What is the growth rate of new subscribers?
